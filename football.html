Football

Rules

  • Objective: The objective of football is to score goals by kicking the ball into the opponent's goal while preventing the opposing team from scoring goals.

  • Teams: Two teams, each consisting of eleven players, compete against each other on a rectangular field called a pitch.

  • Game Duration: A standard football match consists of two halves, each lasting 45 minutes, with a halftime break in between. Additional time, known as stoppage time or injury time, may be added at the end of each half to compensate for time lost due to stoppages in play.

  • Kickoff: The match begins with a kickoff, where one team kicks the ball from the center spot of the field to start play. The opposing team must be positioned outside the center circle until the ball is in play.
